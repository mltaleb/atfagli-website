Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal data collected by Atfagli through our website, mobile applications, and related services (collectively referred to as the “Applications”).

What Data Does Atfagli Collect?

We collect and process the following categories of personal data:

  • Contact Details: Name, email address, phone number, shipping address.
  • Payment Details: Cardholder name, partial credit/debit card details, Stripe, PayPal ID, transaction information.
  • Transaction Details: Shopping purchase history, top-up amounts, and recipient details.
  • Electronic Identifiers: Device information, IP address, geolocation data, cookies, and session logs.
  • Social Media Data: Public profile information, social media handles, and data shared through third-party social media integrations.
  • User Contacts: Contact list data from your device, when explicitly authorized, for facilitating services.
  • Correspondence: Communications with our support team.
  • Marketing Preferences: Information about your preferences and marketing choices.
